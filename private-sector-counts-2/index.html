Private Sector Counts

A tool for seeing where people seek care for fever, ARI, and diarrhea

Health Sector Counts illuminates the role of the public and private sectors in child health. The tool uses Demographic and Health Survey data from 24 USAID maternal and child survival priority countries. Users can view care-seeking patterns for children experiencing three common and treatable illnesses: fever, acute respiratory infection, and diarrhea. Visualizations are available by country and region.

Donors and program implementers have at their fingertips the data they need to design country programs using a total market approach.
