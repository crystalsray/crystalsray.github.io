About Participating

The purpose of the CHAI Study is to understand how your health and well-being compares to the health and wellbeing of military Veterans. Your participation is critically important; your experiences are unique and you cannot be replaced by another person. Your name was randomly selected to represent thousands of others like yourself who considered military service when you did, but decided not to join the military.

The CHAI Web Survey will take about 60 minutes to complete. If you need help completing the survey, feel free to ask a family member or caregiver for help. You can skip any question you do not want to answer.

You will receive $50 for completing the survey.

At the end of the survey you may be invited to participate in one the CHAI Child Study
