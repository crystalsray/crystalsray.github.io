Neurocognitive Assessment

At the end of the survey you may be invited to participate in the CHAI Neurocognitive Assessment. Your participation in the CHAI Neurocognitive Assessment is voluntary. You may choose not to participate in it without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. If you agree to participate in the study, it will be conducted with a trained interviewer in your home or other location that you choose. Each Neurocognitive Assessment participant will receive a $100 check from the interviewer at the end of the assessment.

You will be asked to fill out the HIPAA release and consent forms below during a personal visit if you are selected for the Neurocognitive Assessment.
