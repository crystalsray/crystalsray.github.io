The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is conducting this study of health and well-being to better understand and assist post-9/11 Veterans. The Coming Home from Afghanistan and Iraq (CHAI) Study will provide critical information on the effect of military service on a Veteran later in life. The Department of Veterans Affairs has hired Abt Associates, an independent research company located just outside of Washington, DC, to help conduct this study.

The CHAI Study will focus on three groups of people:

  1. (1) Veterans who deployed to combat zones during the OEF/OIF/OND era;
  2. (2) Veterans who were in the military, but did not deploy to a combat zone during the OEF/OIF/OND era; and
  3. (3) civilians who considered military service, but ended up not joining the military during the OEF/OIF/OND era.
