About Participating

To better serve your generation of Veterans, the VA needs to learn more about your experiences in the military and in civilian life. Your participation is critically important; your experiences are unique and you cannot be replaced by another person. Your name was randomly selected to represent thousands of other post-9/11 Veterans like yourself, and your answers will help the VA understand the health, well-being, and needs of the OEF/OIF/OND Veterans who served between 2001 and 2015.

The survey will take about 60 minutes to complete. You will receive $50 for completing the survey.

At the end of the survey you may be invited to participate in one or both of two related studies:

  1. the CHAI Child Study
  2. the CHAI Neurocognitive Assessment.

If you have more questions about the survey, see descriptions linked to the left, or visit our FAQ page.
