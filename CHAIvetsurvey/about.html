About Participating

The Coming Home from Afghanistan and Iraq (CHAI) survey is an important effort of the VA to learn more about the lives of Veterans who have returned home. You probably received our invitation letter below in the mail recently.

The survey includes three components:

  1. A survey of Veterans
  2. A child survey (if you have children age 12-17), and
  3. A neurocognitive assessment for some respondents

If you have more questions about the survey, see descriptions linked to the left, or visit our FAQ page.
